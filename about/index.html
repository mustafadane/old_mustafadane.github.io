Hey! This is Mustafa. I am a software engineer with a strong passion to coding, learning, and teaching which makes me a constant learner. I like learning new languages, technologies, and libraries. I often find myself reading blog posts about other technologies, watching tutorials, and tinkering with new techs by coding with them!

Currently, I work as a Teaching Fellow at Fullstack Academy of Code in Chicago where I graduated a few months ago. I enjoy teaching coding and algorithms; also weirdly, debugging. I know, right? Sometimes I respect bugs as they take our precious hours where we are dealing with bloody deadlines. And once I found it, I am like uh-huh, there you are! Now, get out of my code! At Fullstack Academy, I daily help students to debug their codes and it is the best feeling when you see the students amazed after finding the bug.

If I am not coding, I might be snowboarding, rollerblading, or flying my drone (In fact, background image of this page was taken by my drone).
